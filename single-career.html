Job Summary

We are seeking an experienced Supply Chain Manager to join our team and oversee the end-to-end supply chain process. This role involves managing the logistics, supplier relationships, and inventory strategies needed to ensure efficient, cost-effective operations. The ideal candidate will bring a strategic approach to drive supply chain excellence and improve our processes.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and implement supply chain strategies aligned with company objectives.
  • Manage supplier relationships and negotiate terms to optimize costs and quality.
  • Oversee inventory levels, warehousing, and logistics to minimize costs and maximize service levels.
  • Coordinate with cross-functional teams to ensure alignment and smooth operations.
  • Monitor KPIs and report on supply chain performance and improvement areas.
  • Identify and mitigate risks within the supply chain to ensure continuity.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Supply Chain Management, Business Administration, or related field.
  • 5+ years of experience in supply chain management or a related role.
  • Strong analytical skills with proficiency in supply chain software and MS Office.
  • Excellent communication and negotiation skills.
  • Proven ability to lead teams and manage cross-functional projects.
  • Strong problem-solving and organizational abilities.

  • Competitive Compensation - Enjoy a comprehensive salary and benefits package.
  • Innovative Solutions - Join a company that values forward-thinking and innovative ideas.
  • Global Impact - Be part of a team impacting the global supply chain industry.
